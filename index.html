Interactive Neurorehabillitation Lab at Virginia Tech

I am a huge HCI (Human-computer interaction) nerd because it is basically the love child of Psychology and Computer Science! I recently started as a part-time undergraduate research intern under Dr. Kelliher. Her research at the rehabillitation lab involves implementing an in-home therapy model to help improve hand-eye coordination amoung stroke patients. I assist the user experience design team with designing the task interface, psychological analysis of optimal patient performence, visualization with stakeholder groups, and contributing to the design of the movement capture and annotation interfaces using Machine Learning.
